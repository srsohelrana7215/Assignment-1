Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Capital markets reform

This includes reviewing regulations to allow more pension funds and insurance companies to invest in stocks.

Banking sector reform

This includes increasing transparency, accountability, and checks and balances in the banking regulator.

Mega-projects

These include the Padma Bridge, the Dhaka Metro Rail, and the Payra Deep Sea Port. and the jamuna setu.

Sustainable infrastructure

This includes physical and social infrastructure, such as healthcare and education facilities.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

His visionary leadership focuses on comprehensive reforms, including restructuring the electoral system, judiciary, and public administration to prevent autocracy, enhance transparency, and foster accountability.
